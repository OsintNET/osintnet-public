# Privacy And Data Handling

OsintNET processes user-provided OSINT targets such as domains, email addresses, usernames, phone numbers, company names, URLs and image files depending on the selected tool.

## Data Users May Submit

- Domain names and IP addresses.
- Search keywords and entities.
- Email addresses and usernames.
- Phone numbers or company names.
- Images for metadata, visual, geolocation or authenticity analysis.
- Website URLs for passive security checks.

## Processing Principles

- Results should be evidence-driven and explainable.
- Sensitive targets should only be submitted when the user has a lawful reason or authorization.
- Passive checks are preferred wherever possible.
- Uploaded image analysis may require temporary processing to extract metadata and forensic signals.

## Third-Party Sources

Some workflows may use external APIs or public web sources for enrichment. Availability and depth can vary by provider, rate limits and configuration.

## User Responsibility

Users are responsible for ensuring they have the right to investigate the submitted targets and for complying with applicable laws and platform terms.
